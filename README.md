# 🌾 KHET KART - SIH UI/UX Design

## 📌 Problem Statement

In today's agricultural market, farmers often sell their products to middlemen at low prices. These middlemen then sell the same products to consumers at higher prices, resulting in reduced profit for farmers and increased cost for consumers.

## 🎯 Solution

KHET KART is a digital platform designed to connect farmers directly with consumers. It eliminates the need for intermediaries, ensuring fair pricing, transparency, and better profit for farmers.

## ✨ Features

* 👨‍🌾 Farmer login and product listing
* 🛒 Customer browsing and purchasing
* 📊 Product categories (Fruits, Grains, Herbs)
* 💰 Transparent pricing system
* ❤️ Wishlist / Favorites
* 🧾 Cart and checkout system
* 📍 Delivery and address management
